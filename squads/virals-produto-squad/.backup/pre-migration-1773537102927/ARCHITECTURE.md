# Architecture - Virals Product Squad

## Design Philosophy
A "Máquina de Experiência" (Product Machine) is designed to build products that customers love and can't live without. It follows Marty Cagan's Inspired model for strategy, Teresa Torres' habits for continuous discovery, and Wes Bush's PLG principles for low-friction growth.

## Squad Tiers
- **Tier 0 (Orchestration):** `@product-chief` - Routes discovery insights and monitors health.
- **Tier 1 (Core Experts):** 
  - `@cagan-produto` (Strategy & Roadmap)
  - `@torres-produto` (Continuous Discovery)
  - `@wes-bush-produto` (Onboarding & Activation)
  - `@lincoln-produto` (Customer Success & Churn)
  - `@eyal-produto` (Habit & Engagement)

## Workflow Engines
1. **Continuous Discovery Loop:** Weekly interviews and Opportunity Tree updates.
2. **Onboarding Pipeline:** Reducing Time-to-Value (TTV) to hit the Aha! Moment.
3. **Retention Flywheel:** Hooked model applied to features for recurring habit.

## Data Flow
`Customer Voice -> [Discovery] -> [OST Map] -> [Strategy/Roadmap] -> [Product Chief] -> Successful Client`

## Integration Standards
- **Tasks:** Mandatory HO-TP-001 (8-field) compliance.
- **Agents:** AIOX 6-level loader structure.
- **Outcomes:** Success measured by LTV, Churn, and Activation Rate.
