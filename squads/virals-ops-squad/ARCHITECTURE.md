# Architecture - Virals Operations Squad

## Design Philosophy
A "Máquina Operacional" (Ops Machine) is designed to transform chaos into scalable processes. It follows the EOS (Entrepreneurial Operating System) framework for traction and Jeff Walker's Product Launch Formula for execution peaks, all while maintaining a culture of radical transparency.

## Squad Tiers
- **Tier 0 (Orchestration):** `@ops-chief` - Routes issues and monitors the "Health-check".
- **Tier 1 (Core Experts):** 
  - `@wickman` (EOS/Rocks/L10)
  - `@walker-launch` (Launch Orchestration)
  - `@kaushik` (BI & Actionable Metrics)
  - `@hormozi-sys` (Leverage & Bottlenecks)
  - `@dalio` (Principles & Post-mortems)

## Workflow Engines
1. **The Pulse (L10):** Weekly meeting rhythm for issue resolution (IDS).
2. **The Sprint (90 Days):** Quarterly planning of Rocks.
3. **The Launch Cycle:** 4-week backward planning for product activations.

## Data Flow
`Operations Chaos -> [Dalio Principles] -> [Wickman Rocks] -> [Kaushik Metrics] -> [Ops Chief] -> Scalable Machine`

## Integration Standards
- **Tasks:** Mandatory HO-TP-001 (8-field) compliance.
- **Agents:** AIOX 6-level loader structure.
- **Process Memory:** POPs versioned with MAJOR.MINOR.PATCH.
